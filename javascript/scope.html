Scope and closures

Scope

Javascript unlike C or C++ (which is kinda looks like) is not block scoped but rather functionally scope. So declare your variables at the top of your functions. That's all I have to say about that.

Closures

Closures are cool and I dont't know if I can really do them justice because I am not the greatest at explaining things. I will do a quick example to sort of demonstrate what I am talking about at least. Functions are fun!
